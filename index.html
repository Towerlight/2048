How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! However, you shall be quick, because there is a time limit!

  • 2 sec: 4 pts
  • 5 sec: 8 pts
  • 10 sec: 16 pts
  • 15 sec: 32 pts
  • 20 sec: 64 pts
  • 40 sec: 128 pts
  • 60 sec: 256 pts
  • 90 sec: 512 pts
  • 180 sec: 1024 pts
  • 600 sec: 2048 pts
